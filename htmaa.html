One Line Summary

Series of projects executed at Fab Lab, Ahmedabad during my 2014 Summer Internship.

Description

I assisted Ar. Urvi Sheth with the Fab Academy course by executing the programming and electronics part of the projects together with Ankita Diwan. We accomplished projects to make smart systems encompassing designing, soldering and programming of electronic boards, experimenting with 3D scanning and printing, CAD/CAM, working with sensors, and making products using casting and molding.

The course provided a broad mental framework to get ideas actualized into objects using quick prototyping.
