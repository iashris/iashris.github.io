The Happiness Project

Deployment

Link

One Line Summary

The Happiness Project aims to quantify happiness and understand the corelation factors through visualization of the analysis of a campus wide survey at IIT Kharagpur with 713 respondents.

Description

Researcher David Sze presents a model of Happiness that we chose to apply in this study. According to Sze, there are three factors that happiness depends on: Life Satisfaction, Moods and Emotions.This tells us that what motivates us to stay happy is the end mental rewards we get in the form of more positive emotions, moods and fulfillment. Hence, we wanted a mathematical term that is blind to external factors like GPA, Department or Friend Circle and only measures the subjective perceptions, moods, and expectations of students. Hence, the Pi Index, a numerical indicator of happiness is introduced keeping these considerations in mind.

Time

March 2016

Collaborators

Ashris, The Scholars' Avenue

The entire documentation of the project along with the interactive visualization charts can be found here: The Happiness Project.

The Scholars' Avenue is the English Newsbody in the campus which I am the Executive Editor of. The study reached our 36k followers and 10k monthly readers across the nation.

Spoiler Alert : The study observed that the kind of friends one keeps and the GPA one achieves are the only strong factors of Happiness in IIT. The clubs, hall of residences, addictions, involvement in relationship, etc played little or no effect in creating a potential happiness difference.
