About Me

Hi, I'm Ashris! I am a designer and a self taught programmer. Since childhood, I have believed that every invention around us is a time capsule containing creativity, intelligence and beauty of human mind iterated over centuries. I wish to be part of this legacy by making products that have a soul in them.

My areas of interest include but are not limited to HCI, Artificial Intelligence, Emotional Technology and New Media Design. I have worked in the field of Cognition driven Architecture (ETH Zurich), Gamification in Architecture (Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden), Algorithmic Planning at CUHK ( Hong Kong ) and Digital Fabrication (Fab Lab, Ahmedabad). I undertake self-initiated time-bound projects to deploy my ideas and learn new skills.

Currently I am pursuing my final year of Bachelor of Architecture at IIT, Kharagpur and am looking forward to be a part of an antidisiplinary team of thinkers who I share my passion of building the future with. Resumé
